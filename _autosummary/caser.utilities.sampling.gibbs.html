caser.utilities.sampling.gibbs

gibbs(in_costs, num_iters, rng=None)[source]

Implements a Gibbs sampler.

Notes

Computationally efficient form of the Metropolis Hastings Markov Chain Monte Carlo algorithm, useful when direct sampling of the target multivariate distribution is difficult. Generates samples by selecting from a histogram constructed from the distribution and randomly sampling from the most likely bins in the histogram. This is based on the sampler in [7].

Parameters:

  • in_costs (N x M numpy array) – Cost matrix.

  • num_iters (int) – Number of iterations to run.

  • rng (numpy random generator, optional) – Random number generator to be used when sampling. The default is None which implies default_rng()

Returns:

  • assignments (M (max size) x N numpy array) – The unique entries from the sampling.

  • costs (M x 1 numpy array) – Cost of each assignment.
